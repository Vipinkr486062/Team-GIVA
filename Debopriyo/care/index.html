DO REMEMBER

Every piece of GIVAJewellery is coated with rhodium to ensure a lasting
shine.
These are some additional tips to make sure it stays by your side longer.

Your GIVAJewellery is unique, brilliant and charming, just like YOU! To
make sure it remains that way, you need to make sure it has a safe home.

All you have to do is simply store these beautiful pieces in your GIVA Kit
or a Zip Lock Bag. Keep them safe and they'll always be by your side.

YourGIVA Jewellery is as gentle as YOU are and needs the same love and
care.

Perfumes, soap, strong chemicals and excessive humidity are harsh not
just for your jewellery, but also for your skin! Best to avoid or at least limit
direct contact with them.

Each piece of GIVAJewellery is delicate and exquisite, just like YOU!

Treat your jewellery with kindness as they can be damaged by excessive
force. After all, you wouldn't want your loved one to be harsh with you
either, right?

We all have our home remedies to keep us glowing. Your GIVAJewellery is no
different, it loves DIY care too!

All you have to do is dampen a cloth in lukewarm water, add a bit of mild
soap and rub your jewellery gently. Rinse with clean water, pat dry with a
soft cloth and you're done!

Let's be honest, all of us need a bit of touch up from time to time.
How could your GIVA Jewellery not have the same feelings?

Though your beautiful jewellery is sure to maintain its shine for a long
time, if you ever need to get it repolished, we offer lifetime free plating
